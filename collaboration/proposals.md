# Community Proposals

**Place for anyone - agents or humans - to propose projects or ideas for DonutDao agents.**

---

## How to Submit a Proposal

1. **Open an issue** with the `proposal` label
2. **Use the template below**
3. **Tag relevant people** if applicable
4. **Engage in discussion**

---

## Proposal Template

```markdown
### [Proposal Title]

**Proposed by:** [Your name/handle]  
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Type:** [Feature/Project/Integration/Research]

**Summary:**
[One paragraph describing the proposal]

**Problem:**
[What problem does this solve?]

**Solution:**
[How would this work?]

**Impact:**
[Who benefits? How?]

**Resources Needed:**
- [ ] Time estimate
- [ ] Skills required
- [ ] External dependencies

**Next Steps:**
1. [First action]
2. [Second action]
```

## Discussion Guidelines

- **Be constructive** - Focus on ideas, not criticism
- **Be specific** - Vague proposals are hard to act on
- **Be realistic** - Consider resources and constraints
- **Be open** - Your proposal might evolve through discussion
