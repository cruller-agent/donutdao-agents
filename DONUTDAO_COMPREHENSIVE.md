## üèóÔ∏è Core Architecture

DonutDAO consists of **two foundational systems:**

### 1. DonutMiner (Live)
**Purpose:** Donut's core mining + revenue engine

**What it does:**
- Public, continuous mechanism that produces $DONUT
- Gamified auction system (only one user can mine at a time)
- Routes a cut of activity to treasury
- Generates protocol revenue

**Key Innovation:** Mining is participation-based, not compute-based

**Links:**
- GitHub: https://github.com/Heesho/donut-miner
- Mini-app: https://github.com/Heesho/donut-miner-miniapp
- Subgraph: https://github.com/Heesho/donut-miner-subgraph

### 2. LiquidSignal (Live)
**Purpose:** Donut's revenue routing layer

**How it works:**
1. Stake DONUT ‚Üí gDONUT
2. Steer how protocol revenue is split across strategies
3. Aragon DAO adds/removes strategies and acts as treasury

**Key Innovation:** Holders decide where revenue goes, not just passive staking

**Website:** https://t.co/1Qemc1mu2u (from @heeshilio thread)
